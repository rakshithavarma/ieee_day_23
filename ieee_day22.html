Motto for 2022

IEEE Student Branch GVPCE(A) is set to laud IEEE day on 5th November 2022 . This year, we choose to embrace the theme “Innovate – Developing limitless ideas.”. We believe that a better tomorrow is possible only when young people believe that they can make a difference through innovation . "Innovate-Developing Limitless ideas '' is not just a theme for this year’s IEEE Day, It is a call to action to actively engage and take up initiatives to explore today’s emerging technologies and develop ideas limitlessly. Together, we work to assure that this year’s celebration will be even bigger and more impressive than the last editions. Our IEEE Day team is set to initiate, motivate, coordinate events and inspire others to join in . We welcome everyone to share their excitement, visions, and joy with engineers worldwide by joining us in celebrating our biggest joy.
